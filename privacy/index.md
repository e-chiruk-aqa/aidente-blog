**Effective Date: 14/09/2025**

AIDente, developed and operated by Yauheni Chyruk ("we," "our," or "us"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

- We do not require you to create an account.
- If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address.

### 1.2 Usage Data

- We may collect non-identifiable data such as app usage statistics, device type, and crash logs to improve performance.
- We may use third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Amplitude, Apphud, Apple Analytics, Railway Logs, Facebook) for analytics and error tracking. These services may collect information such as device identifiers, app interactions, and purchase events.

### 1.3 Nutrition Data

- Information you input (e.g., food names, weights, or photos) is used only to provide nutritional calculations.
- This data may be processed by third-party services such as OpenAI API to generate calorie and macro results. Data is transmitted securely and not used for other purposes.

## 2. How We Use Information

- To provide and improve app functionality.
- To respond to customer inquiries.
- To analyze app performance, improve user experience, monitor subscriptions, and fix bugs.
- To collect aggregated analytics through third-party services such as Firebase, Amplitude, Apphud, Apple Analytics, and others.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data only in the following cases:

- When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
- With trusted service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., analytics, error tracking, and performance monitoring). These providers may receive limited information such as device identifiers, app usage events, and purchase events, strictly for the purpose of analytics and app improvement.

## 4. Payments & Subscriptions

Payments for premium features and subscriptions are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details.

## 5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Storage & Retention

- Nutrition and meal data you enter are stored locally on your device and not uploaded to our servers.
- If you delete the App, your locally stored data is also removed.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR/CCPA), you may have the right to:

- Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
- Restrict or object to processing of your data.
- Withdraw consent at any time.
